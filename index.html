Our Featured Destinations

Gulmarg - Meadow of Flowers

Famous for its pristine snowy slopes, Gulmarg hosts the highest operational Gondola in the world, offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas. A haven for skiing and winter sports.

Sonmarg - Meadow of Gold

A stunning valley nestled amidst towering glaciers and lakes. It serves as a base camp for several mountain treks and is a gateway to Ladakh.

Pahalgam - Valley of Shepherds

Known for the Lidder River and picturesque valleys like Betaab and Aru. It's the perfect spot for quiet relaxation, fishing, and pony rides.

Srinagar - Heart of Kashmir

Home to the iconic Dal Lake, Mughal Gardens, and Houseboats. Srinagar blends natural beauty with rich cultural heritage and history.
